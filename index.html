Executive Perspective

THE COLLECTIVE.

A creative collective of 35 designers and garment artisans dedicated to crafting the next generation of high-fashion silhouettes through structural innovation.

Creative Director

Mia Zhang

Founder of SoroFib / Featuring Sorona® Technology

Strategic Origins Born in Taipei and educated at the National University of Singapore, Mia developed a synthesis of design aesthetics and institutional business systems.

Global Operations Over 9 years of industry tenure, including a 5-year tenure as Sales Director for a multinational family business, specializing in strategic channel expansion.

Post-COVID Pivot Transitioning to the US, she expanded her focus to material investment and the integration of bio-based fibers like Sorona® into future textile trends.

Current Mandate As Creative Director, Mia leads the creative vision, ensuring every silhouette reflects a harmony between avant-garde aesthetics and material excellence.
